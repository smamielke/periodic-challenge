Core Concepts

  • Atoms: The basic building blocks of matter.
  • Elements: Pure substances made of one type of atom.
  • Bonds: The way atoms connect to form molecules.
  • Reactions: When atoms rearrange to form new substances.
  • Energy: The power behind all chemical change.
